About Us

Montane Shipping incorporated in 2004 has PAN India presence and is a multi-modal transport operator, freight forwarder and logistics company. Our expert team is stationed at all major seaports, airports, inland container depot providing a full range of logistic services be it custom clearance, warehousing, transshipment, re-forwarding, domestic distribution and a plethora of services, custom made to meet the specific needs of various customers.

Our planning, co-ordination teams devise, monitor, time the execution of services, be it on the sea, air, road and rail. Customer care, attention and satisfaction coupled with quality and cost-effectiveness are our major principles. We are committed to offering the best possible solutions and services to the EXIM Community by aligning specific requirements with affordable costs, without compromising on the rules, regulations to ensure seamless transportation of the goods.

Competent, affordable pricing even while offering outstanding services is our USP and we at Montane, ensure it round-the-clock, every day.

Our Mission
