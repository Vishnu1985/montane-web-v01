Our Strength

We have a team of over 50 associates adequately skilled, highly motivated and well trained to handle all circumstances

The Vision
Montane is a visionary when it comes to offering worldwide logistics and supply chain management solutions. We have remained as a trusted partner for a countless number of clients for more than 15 years. For us, success and loyalty to customers are of paramount importance and we relentlessly work towards being the Numero Uno, providing turnkey solutions coupled with innovation and reliability.

The Mission
Montane is growing strength to strength and we strongly believe in exceptional teamwork, coordination, planning, positivity as important factors that would lead the clients and us towards success. Positive feedback from the customers pumps us with a lot of energy, builds our hopes and helps us towards developing an optimistic outlook towards improving our services.

Montane Shipping incorporated in 2004 has PAN India presence and is a multi-modal transport operator, freight forwarder and logistics company. Our expert team is stationed at all major seaports, airports, inland container depot providing a full range of logistic services be it custom clearance, warehousing, transshipment, re-forwarding, domestic distribution and a plethora of services, custom made to meet the specific needs of various customers.

Our planning, co-ordination teams devise, monitor, time the execution of services, be it on the sea, air, road and rail. Customer care, attention and satisfaction coupled with quality and cost-effectiveness are our major principles. We are committed to offering the best possible solutions and services to the EXIM Community by aligning specific requirements with affordable costs, without compromising on the rules, regulations to ensure seamless transportation of the goods.

Competent, affordable pricing even while offering outstanding services is our USP and we at Montane, ensure it round-the-clock, every day.

Montane Shipping Company was set up with an idea to provide industry oriented supply chain solutions via sea, air, rail and road freight. Over the years, we have handled the toughest projects which helped us in empowering, learning and in ensuring the best services to our customers.

Our services are cost-effective and we offer reliable guidance in choosing the mode of transport through all possible routes based on the urgency of the shipment.

We at Montane constantly keep a tab on constantly changing environmental conditions to ensure hassle-free delivery of the goods. Our large network of partners spread across the length and breadth of every continent ensure support while dealing with international operations. Our goal at Montane Shipping is to be ‘World’s Leading Logistics service Provider’ offering the best solutions to the customers.
